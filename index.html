About me

Alicia is a data scientist and aspiring full-stack MLOps engineer based out of Raleigh, NC with a background in healthcare analytics, health intervention research, and mental health. In her free time, she enjoys rock climbing, reading, lifting, meditating, and hiking.

She received her MS in Analytics from North Carolina State University's Institute for Advanced Analytics in 2021, and her BA in Psychology from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2016. She currently works at Evernorth as a data scientist using health claims to build out predictive models to identify high cost claimants and behavioral conditions.

Prior to Evernorth, Alicia worked at VitalFlo, a small lung health analytics startup, building out predictive models doing machine learning work to predict asthma exacerbation.

Alicia does take on software development, data science and statistical consulting projects, so feel free to reach out and contact her for a quote. Past clients have included those in healthcare, education, and others. She enjoys working with startups, small business, and larger organizations as well.
